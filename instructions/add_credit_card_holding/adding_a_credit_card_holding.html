Adding a Credit Card Holding

Home

Introduction

This guide explains how to add a credit card holding to your account.

Steps

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the 'Holdings' section.
  3. Click on 'Add New Holding'.
  4. Select 'Credit Card' as the holding type.
  5. Enter your credit card details:
    • Card Name (e.g., Personal Visa, Business Amex)
    • Card Number
    • Expiry Date
    • CVV
    • Credit Limit
    • Current Balance
    • Statement Closing Date
    • Payment Due Date
  6. Review the information and click 'Save'.

Your credit card holding should now be added to your account. You can view and manage it from the 'Holdings' section.

Important Notes

  • Ensure all information entered is accurate to avoid any issues.
  • For security reasons, do not share your full credit card number or CVV with anyone unnecessarily.
  • You can typically edit or remove your credit card holding later if needed.
